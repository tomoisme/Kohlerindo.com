Aritco 4000

Explore the Aritco 4000

Our smallest and most compact lift. The lift has been designed to meet all requirements for comfort, space and design. The lift is available in different sizes, with an option to personalise it with details such as glass walls, a range of varied colours and different materials.

Contact Sales

Key Features

  • Customisable with more than 200 different colours, 4 different glass types and 9 different floors.
  • Very compact and space efficient which makes it possible to install the lift in almost any house, even when space is limited.
  • Equipped with our SmartSafety system, with safety features, to meet all situations that can occur in a home, as well as to prevent accidents.
